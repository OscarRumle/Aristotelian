Description
The Crown of the Sundered Reach is cast from dark iron threaded with gold, its two halves joined at a jagged seam that was never meant to close again. The left half bears the sigil of the old kings; the right is plain, as if waiting for a name to be engraved. It is heavier than it looks.
Provenance
Forged in the first age of the Reach, split by the king's own hand during the siege of the capital. One half was buried with him in the vaults beneath the old citadel. The other half vanished — some say stolen, some say hidden, some say it walked away on its own.
Dramatic weight
The crown does not grant authority — it only makes a claim. To hold it is to inherit everything the old kingdom did wrong. Caela Vorne has been told all her life that the crown is her birthright; she has not been told what it costs to wear it.
Signature line
"Every king who ever wore it whole died on a blade held by someone they trusted."
