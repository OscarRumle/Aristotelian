Description
The Order of the Sealed Compact presents itself as a brotherhood of mediators — learned men who travel between the great houses to prevent bloodshed through negotiation and contract law. In practice they have become the most sophisticated information network in the Reach, and every contract they broker contains a clause no one reads.
History
Founded three hundred years ago by a treaty-writer who grew tired of watching wars restart the moment his ink dried, the Compact began as a genuine attempt to build lasting peace through enforceable law. Somewhere in the second century it discovered that information was more durable than any treaty — and more profitable.
Dramatic role
The Compact embodies the corruption of good intentions. It genuinely believes it prevents more wars than it causes. The question the story asks is whether that calculation is correct — and who bears the cost of being wrong. Duke Orvassen has been their most reliable client for fifteen years. Caela Vorne does not yet know this.
Internal tension
They see themselves as neutral. They have not been neutral in decades.
